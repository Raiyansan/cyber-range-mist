MIST Cyber Range

A Hub of Training and Exellence in Cyber Security

♦
Explore Cyber Range

Virtual Tour

✻

MIST Cyber Range is a world-class state-of-the-art facility under the supervision of the Dept of CSE, MIST for enabling hyper-realistic hands-on training on modern cyber security. The range was sponsored by the ICT Division, Govt. of the People's Republic of Bangladesh and it houses modern devices, equipment, and technology from Cyberbit, Singapore.

Being the first academic institute in Bangladesh to proudly host such a range, MIST is committed to providing exceptional training, consultancy, and auditing services in cyber security. The immersive simulated environment of networks, traffic, and attack scenarios is the immaculate setup that’s needed for effective training in cyber security. Be a part of this hub of training and excellence and take your career in cyber security to the next height!

Our Services

✻

MIST Cyber Range is committed to becoming a hub of training and excellence in cyber security. In this journey, our services range from Training, Consultancy for security systems, security testing, and auditing.

Training

We provide hands-on training for beginners, enthusiasts and professionals in cyber security.

Consultancy

Consultancy services are available in Information Systems Security Architecture, System Design, Risk Assessment, and Cyber threat & Malware Analysis.

Testing

We are also committed to providing ideal Vulnerability Analysis, Penetration Testing, and Cyber and Financial Information Systems Auditing.
