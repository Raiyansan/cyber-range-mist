Instructor 01

A B M Ahasan Ullah

✻

CISA, CDPSE, C|CISO, PMP, PCIP, CPISI, CDCP, CDCS, CDMS, CND, CEH, ECSA, CHFI, CCNA, CCNP, RHCE, ISO/IEC 27001:2013 LA, LI

A skilled IT professional with 16 years of experience specializing in IT security. He started his career with a nationwide internet service provider in Bangladesh as a network engineer and gradually created a presence in the IT industry as a security expert. He has obtained international exposure through working with some international clients and attending numerous international training and workshops in IT Security.

His key strength areas include Design & implementation of Enterprise Network, IT Infrastructure Management, Governance of Enterprise IT, IT Security & Risk Management, Systems & Virtualization Technology, Project Management, Implementation of Information System Security Standards, Payment Security, Information System Audit, Computer Forensic, Ethical Hacking, IT Human Resource Management, and financial planning for IT. He was the core member for some of the major projects completed in recent years, including implementation of the PCI DSS and ISO/IEC 27001 to meet the compliance requirements for the organization. He was the team lead for implementing the data center, BCP-DRP project, and virtualization projects for his current organization. He has obtained his bachelor degree in Computer Science & Engineering from International Islamic University Chittagong and a Master's degree in Computer Science & Engineering major in Cyber Security from United International University, Dhaka Bangladesh.
